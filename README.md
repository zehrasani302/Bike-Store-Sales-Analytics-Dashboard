# Bike Store Sales Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This interactive Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of a bike store's sales performance. It enables business stakeholders to monitor revenue, evaluate customer purchasing behavior, analyze product category performance, and identify top-performing stores and staff through interactive visualizations.

---

## Business Problem

Retail businesses require real-time insights into sales performance to make informed decisions regarding inventory management, customer retention, and operational efficiency.

This dashboard was developed to consolidate key performance metrics into a single interactive report, allowing users to quickly identify trends, monitor KPIs, and evaluate business performance.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze overall sales performance
- Monitor revenue and profitability indicators
- Identify top-performing product categories
- Evaluate store and staff performance
- Track customer purchasing behavior
- Support data-driven business decisions

---

## Key Insights

- Generated over **$8M** in total revenue.
- Achieved a **99.9% Repeat Purchase Rate**, indicating strong customer loyalty.
- Cruisers recorded the highest quantity sold among all bike categories.
- Store 2 contributed the highest share of overall revenue.
- Revenue remained relatively consistent throughout the week.
- Discounted sales accounted for approximately **10.37%** of total revenue.
- Category sales velocity highlighted differences in product demand across bike segments.
